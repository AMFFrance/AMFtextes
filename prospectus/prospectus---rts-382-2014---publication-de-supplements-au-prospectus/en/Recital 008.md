(8) Information concerning the identity of the main shareholders or any controlling entity of the issuer is vital for an informed assessment of the issuer, in case of any type of security. However, a situation of a change of control of the issuer is particularly significant where the offer refers to equity securities and depository receipts as these types of securities are, in general, more price sensitive to this situation. Therefore, a supplement should be published where there is a change of control of an issuer of equity securities or an issuer of underlying shares in the case of depository receipts.
